Welcome to Chrome Time Tracker

Chrome Time Tracker automatically monitors how much time you spend on different websites, helping you understand your browsing habits.

What it does:

  • Automatically tracks time spent on websites
  • Only counts time when Chrome is active
  • Provides calendar-based statistics
  • Stores data locally on your computer

How to use:

Click the Chrome Time Tracker icon in your toolbar to view your browsing statistics. Select any day from the calendar to see detailed time breakdowns.
